Donut Clicker

Click the donut clicker button to make a donut.

The more you click, the more you make. Be sure to drink plenty of fluids and stretch so you dont strain your finger!!!

Creates a single Donut per click until you Purchase AutoClickers and DonutMultiplers

[E.G. 1 click creates 1 donut] (Cost: Free)

Increase the value of a click by 20% per Multiplier

[E.G. 1 click becomes 1.2 clicks] (Cost: 10 donuts)

Applies an AutoClicker that clicks on your behalf every second per AutoClicker

[E.G. 2 AutoClickers will click for you 2 times per second] (Cost: 100 Donuts)

This will reset the game
